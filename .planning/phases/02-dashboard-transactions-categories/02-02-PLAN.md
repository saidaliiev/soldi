|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-02-02-01 | Tampering | dashboardRepo.getDailyExpenseTotals | mitigate | Parameterized `?` placeholders; date bounds validated as ISO 8601 strings via `new Date(x).toISOString()` round-trip before binding; reject `NaN` dates (throw, caught by useDigestData try/catch) |
